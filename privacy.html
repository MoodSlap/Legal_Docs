Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MoodSlap ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

Summary: MoodSlap is designed with privacy in mind. We do not sell your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Personal Data

We do not collect or store personally identifiable information (PII) such as your real name, address, or phone number on our servers. The name you enter into the App ("User Name") is stored locally on your device and synchronized via your personal Apple iCloud account. We do not have access to this data.

1.2. Usage Data & Analytics

We use third-party analytics services (such as Firebase Analytics) to understand how the App is used and to improve our Service. This data is anonymized and may include:

  • Device type and operating system version.
  • Feature usage statistics (e.g., how often a character is selected).
  • Crash reports and performance data.

1.3. Location Information

MoodSlap requests access to your device's location ("Approximate" or "Precise") solely to provide local weather-based content.

  • Local Processing Only: Your coordinates are processed locally on your device to fetch weather data.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your location history, store your coordinates on our servers, or share your location with third parties for advertising purposes.
  • Weather Service: Location data is sent ephemerally to Apple WeatherKit to retrieve current weather conditions.

1.4. Calendar Data

MoodSlap may request access to your device's calendar to import events as tasks.

  • Local Processing Only: Calendar events are read locally on your device and stored via your personal Apple iCloud account.
  • No Server Storage: We do not store your calendar data on our servers or share it with third parties.
  • Optional Feature: Calendar access is entirely optional and only activated when you choose to import events.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service.
  • To synchronize your progress across devices using Apple iCloud.
  • To monitor usage patterns and detect technical issues.
  • To provide localized weather phrase generation via Apple WeatherKit.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

All financial transactions are handled directly by Apple via the App Store. We do not process, store, or have access to your credit card or payment information.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you (by device ID, not personal name). Link to privacy policies of third-party service providers used by the app:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Apple Services
  • Apple WeatherKit

5. Disclaimer regarding AI Characters

The characters in MoodSlap (including but not limited to the "Psychologist" and "Fitness Coach") are fictional AI personas designed for entertainment purposes only. They do not provide professional medical, psychological, legal, or financial advice. Interactions should not be considered a substitute for professional consultation.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from anyone under the age of 13 without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
